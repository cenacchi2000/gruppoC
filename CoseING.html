Print Fleet Menagement

All of the benefits of this solution are represented by a number of Cloud software integrated with all Computer Center dedicated printer management services.

All of these software and services dedicated to the printing world are called the Print Fleet Management (PFM) solution. This solution is a decisive help to control costs in single or dislocated environments, centralizing the responsibility for purchasing, installing, maintaining, designing, and supporting your imaging and printing resources.

The Computer Center Print Fleet Management solution helps increase staff performance and at the same time reduce total cost of ownership (TCO). The Print Fleet Management project is a goal to reduce costs and accustom users to use digitized documents in various standard formats. Reducing costs is an imperative for businesses to try to deliver an effective response.

This variable adds to the degree of complexity that, with equal volumes, the costs may still vary. The processes and organization of each enterprise have a direct and different impact on every reality to be improved. The strategic superiority of cost management is perhaps difficult to accept, but it is unmistakable: there is no risk of a short-term advantage stemming from a generalized cost-cutting maneuver, compromising the competitive success of a project or enterprise over time.

Why you should choose us?

Our services intervene in an outstanding manner as compared to the other organizative aspects related to the use of printers, their maintenance and management. This permits a major operative and productive efficience with a reduced costs visible especially hidden costs related to the study of TCO (Total Cost of Ownership).

We offer the client 30 years of experience combined with simple and complete instruments, which is able to monitor and analize the consumation and savings. On the printers it will be possible to it will be possible to set up Business policies such as the release of the machine front output, rules regarding the color-use, determine jobs accounting based on the client's needs or that of the machine.

What is PFM?

It is an innovative solution for “Pay Per Use” that semplifies and optimizes the management of every printer suitable to specific work needs, obtaining a cost-support based on real-time consumption.

Through a set of Cloud software, integrated with the Centro Computer services, that demand the monitoring of printing procceses from our Team, freeing the client from any type of problem related to the malfunctioning and the maintenance of the equipment.

Why you should choose us

Our Thirty Years of experience in this sector allows us to guide the client towards the best solution. We offer the possibility of a complete that goes through thorough analisis, control of printers up to the possibility of a remote management of an automatic provision of consumables.

Simple but complete intstruments are always available to the client, that are always able to generate any type of report to monitor and analise consumation and savings. On the Printers, it will be possible to set up Business policies such as the release of the machine front output, rules regarding the color-use, determine jobs accounting based on the client's needs or that of the machine.
